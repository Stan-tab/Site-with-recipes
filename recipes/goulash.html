So let`s start cooking GOULASH

First of all we should talk about the products that we will use:

  • Beans - 500gr
  • Meat - 1kg
  • Carrot - 300-400
  • Paprika - 3pc (250gr each)
  • Garlic - 8-16
  • Reddish ginger - 2 tablespoons(20-80gr)
  • Tomato - 500gr
  • Onions - 500gr
  • Celery stalk - 200gr(if you wish)
  • Tomato Paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Wet both - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Pepper - 1 teaspoon

You can watch this videoinstruction:

  1. Soak the beans in boiling water while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Cut the meat into large cubes. Chop the peppers, tomatoes and celery randomly.
  3. Chop the onions, garlic and carrots. Finely chop the ginger.
  4. Start frying the meat in the pan until crispy. Then add the onions, fry some more and after that add the carrots.
  5. Cook for a couple more minutes and add the peppers and celery. Then add the tomato paste. Don't forget to stir constantly during all the steps.
  6. When the tomato paste is heated add the garlic and ginger, stir well.
  7. Last add the beans and pour the broth over everything, so that the water covers everything by 2-3 cm from the top.
  8. Turn the heat down to low and leave for at least an hour under a lid. Then add salt and pepper.

Done!
